Fantastic Smoothies was founded just 3 years ago and has already become one of the most successful smoothie brands in Springfield, MO. Thanks to this rapid success, we are currently looking to expand to the rest of Missouri.

Chris de Leon, Founder and CEO

Chris grew up on a fruit farm in southern Missouri. From an early age, he developed a love of all things involving fruit, especially his family's homemade smoothie recipies. When he grew up, he knew that what he wanted to do with his life was to share the joy those smoothies brought to him with the rest of the world.

James Gregory Falge, Co-Founder and CFO

James grew up in a relatively normal household in central Missouri. He studied finance in college and hoped it would be a way to help other people achieve financial security. Working at Missouri's first national bank, however, the only thing James proved was the existance of the living dead, the job having killed him on the inside. So, when Chris entered his bank looking for a loan, James jumped at the chance to do something he would actually find joy in.
